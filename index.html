Why are we here?

The mission...

Technology is one of the world's greatest resources. Too often, however, only those who have the convenience to be around technology get to experience the joy of using and cultivating it. We are going to change that. Our mission is to provide access to technology to those who donâ€™t typically have exposure to it. Everyone, no matter their circumstance, should be able to share in the joy and fulfillment that technology brings to everyone.

We plan to do this by bringing technology such as myros, circuit boards, drones, go-pros, virtual reality headsets and more to children's hospitals, orphanages, nursing homes, veterans, and homeless shelters. The goal is to spread the love and joy for technology. Our tech-loving staff will visit these places and display our gadgets. Our team will connect with people one on one to build relationships and foster learning.

Next

Being a resource for further growth.

Anyone can be a Hacker With Heart. We believe that as long as one has the eagerness to learn and the fascination to grow, they can master anything. In the beginning, our Hackers will simply demo our technology and demonstrate the power of learning how to code. When someone demonstrates an interest in furthering their own learning, we then become a medium through which that becomes possible. We then will work closely with those we visit to provide the online resources and guidance necessary to teach themselves how to code.
